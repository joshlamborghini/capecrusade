Forming a Team:

Teams consist of 5 kiters.

All levels of experience are welcomed to join the fun - You simply need to be able to ride up wind! We encourage you to form teams with your friends, however we can help individuals connect with other kiters if need. Just let us know !

Registration is limited to 30 teams, so register today!

Registration Fee:

Required fundraising minimum of $1,000 per team.

All Registered racers receive:

  • Crusade Race Jersey
  • Invitation to the post-race award party with food buffet
  • Team fundraising page
  • Online fundraising tool kit
  • A customizable team page to collect pledges from family, friends and co-workers to sponsor your team

Prizes:

Prizes are awarded to the top fundraising team and the top placing team in the race. Have fun with it, and remember it's all for a great cause!

We will also be hosting at least 1 registration/sign up party this spring. We are planning a fun night out where you will be able to sign up and form teams. Right now we are thinking it will be a night at an indoor paintball arena to also include a gear swap! Stay tuned!
