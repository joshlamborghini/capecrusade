Cape Cod's Premiere Charity Kiteboarding Race

Mission:

Kiteboarders harness the elements for sport, but we all take much more from the beaches we visit. Call it mental clarify or spiritual, energy or renewal, for all of us it is therapeutic. We challenge our community to help share these benefits with those who have survived cancer.

The Cape Crusade endeavors to support the amazing work that Athlete’s 4 Cancer started back in 2007. Athletes 4 Cancer’s mission is to renew, rebuild, and restart lives after cancer through outdoor adventure. The success of cancer research through the years has led to a greater need to focus on survivorship as more patients are being treated effectively and living longer from diagnosis. Athletes 4 Cancer draws patients in remission aged 18-39 from across the country to participate in their programs.

In addition to supporting Athletes 4 Cancer 20% of proceeds will be donated to a local Cape Cod Charity. Working locally and having an impact globally is at the core of our mission.

Participation:

There are many ways to participate in this great event, as a racer, sponsor, spectator, or volunteer! The event centers around a 26 mile relay race but is sure to have activities for people of all ages/abilities!

Forming a Team:

Teams will consist of 5 kiters. All levels of experience are welcomed to join the fun. All you need is to be able to ride up wind! We encourage you to form teams with your friends to add to the fun, however if you need help finding others to participate with let us know and we will help you connect with other kites.

Registration is free with a team fundraising minimum of $1,000.

All Registered racers receive:

  • Crusade Race Jersey
  • Invitation to the post-race award party with food buffet
  • Team fundraising page
  • Online fundraising tool kit
  • A customizable team page to collect pledges from family, friends and coworkers to sponsor your team, as well as a tool kit to help you reach these fundraising goals.

Keep in mind, prizes will also be awarded to the top fundraising team! So have fun with it, and remember its all for a great cause!

Beach Support:

Want to participate but don’t feel up to the race? We still need your support! We will need plenty of support on race day. If you are interested in volunteering please send us an email (weblink) and we will find a fun way for you to get involved and help the cause. You can cheer on your friends and be an important part of making this event a success!

Post Race Party:

After the race we will be hosting a little soiree to include food, drinks, silent auction, raffle and prizes for our race winners and top fundraisers. Entry is free for all racers/volunteers, additional tickets can be purchased $15 in advance and $20 at the door. Come join us for a great night, win some prizes and feel good doing it!
